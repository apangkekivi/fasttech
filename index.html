About Us

FAST TECH EDUCATION FOUNDATION is a computer training institude offering wide range of courses. The institute is registered under Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA), Govt of India, MSME and ISO certified 9001:2015.

FAST TECH aims to privides students with an opportunity to gain accredited, marketable works skills which in turn can lead to employment opportunities. We also aim at providing a foundation on which students can build new skills for the job and eventually help them to prepare for a wide variety of possible careers and joining classes based on computer education.
